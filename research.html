4.
Limited Risk Transfer Between Investors: A New Benchmark for Macro-Finance Models
with Xavier Gabaix, Ralph Koijen, Federico Mainardi, and Motohiro Yogo [SSRN] (December 2024)
Risk transfer (how much risk changes hands) is small: 0.65% per quarter. Standard models, however, predict 10x more. A new model with inelastic demand provides reconciliation.
• Presentations: CEPR Gerzensee (Scheduled), 4th Annual Holden Conference in Finance and Real Estate (Scheduled), Demand in Asset Markets (DAM) Working Group, NBER SI Household Finance 2024
